Frequently asked questions

My swims are not showing up

Check that HealthKit permission is enabled for SwimTrace (Health app > Sharing > Apps) and that your swim workouts exist in the Health app.

My pace looks different from the Workout app

SwimTrace shows net pace, which removes rest between sets. This is expected, and usually reflects your real swimming speed more closely.

How do I manage a subscription?

If you subscribed through the App Store, billing, renewal, and cancellation are managed in your Apple account subscription settings.

常见问题

为什么看不到我的游泳记录？

请确认已为 SwimTrace 开启 HealthKit 权限（“健康”应用 > 共享 > App），并且你的游泳训练记录已存在于“健康”应用中。

为什么配速和「体能训练」不一样？

SwimTrace 显示的是净配速，会去掉组间休息。这是预期行为，通常更贴近你真正的游泳速度。

如何管理订阅？

如果你通过 App Store 订阅，续费、取消和账单管理需要在 Apple 账户的订阅设置中完成。
