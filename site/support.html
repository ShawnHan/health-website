English

Frequently asked questions

Health data is not showing up

Please check that HealthKit permission is enabled for Recova and that the relevant data exists in the Health app.

Recommendations look incomplete

Training load and recovery guidance depends on available HealthKit records such as workouts, heart rate, sleep, and related metrics.

How do I manage a subscription?

If you subscribed through the App Store, subscription billing, renewal, and cancellation are managed through your Apple account subscription settings.

中文

常见问题

为什么看不到健康数据？

请确认已为 Recova 开启 HealthKit 权限，并且相关数据已经存在于“健康”应用中。

为什么建议内容不完整？

运动负荷和恢复建议依赖可用的 HealthKit 数据，例如运动记录、心率、睡眠和其他相关指标。

如何管理订阅？

如果你通过 App Store 订阅，订阅的续费、取消和账单管理需要在 Apple 账户的订阅设置中完成。
