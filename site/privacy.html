Overview

SwimTrace is a health and fitness app for pool swimmers. It reads selected HealthKit data to calculate swim performance metrics (such as net pace, SWOLF, distance, and per-stroke splits) and recovery context (such as readiness, HRV, and sleep). SwimTrace does not require account creation or login.

Contact email: support@swimtrace.app

概述

SwimTrace 是一款面向泳池游泳者的健康与健身应用。它会读取部分 HealthKit 数据，用于计算游泳表现指标（如净配速、SWOLF、距离、分泳姿配速）和运动恢复信息（如准备度、HRV、睡眠）。SwimTrace 不要求创建账号或登录。

联系邮箱：support@swimtrace.app

1. Data SwimTrace reads

With your permission, SwimTrace may read the following HealthKit data:

  • Swimming distance
  • Swimming laps / lengths
  • Swimming stroke style
  • Stroke count
  • Pool length
  • Heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Sleep analysis
  • Workouts
  • Active energy burned

1. 读取哪些数据

在你授权后，SwimTrace 可能读取以下 HealthKit 数据：

  • 游泳距离
  • 游泳圈数 / 趟数
  • 泳姿
  • 划水次数
  • 池长
  • 心率
  • 心率变异性（HRV）
  • 静息心率
  • 睡眠分析
  • 训练记录
  • 活动能量

2. How data is processed and stored

All analysis happens on your device. SwimTrace does not operate its own server, and your health data is not uploaded to or stored by the developer.

SwimTrace does not include third-party analytics, advertising, social login, or crash-reporting SDKs.

If an Apple Watch companion is used, data is shared locally between the iPhone and Watch through an App Group on your devices; it is not sent to any external service.

2. 数据如何处理与存储

所有分析都在你的设备上完成。SwimTrace 不运营自有服务器，你的健康数据不会被上传到开发者处，也不会由开发者保存。

SwimTrace 未接入第三方统计、广告、社交登录或崩溃统计 SDK。

如使用 Apple Watch 配套端，数据通过设备上的 App Group 在 iPhone 与 Watch 之间本地共享，不会发送到任何外部服务。

3. What we do not collect

SwimTrace does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, or other identifying profile information. It does not track your location, and it does not build advertising or marketing profiles.

3. 不收集什么

SwimTrace 不需要账户，也不收集你的姓名、邮箱或其他可识别身份的资料。它不追踪你的位置，也不会构建广告或营销画像。

4. Subscriptions and payment

5. Your rights and choices

  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in the Health app under Sharing > Apps.
  • Uninstalling SwimTrace removes the app and the data it stored on your device.
  • You can turn off notifications in iOS Settings at any time.

5. 你的权利与选择

  • 你可以随时在“健康”应用的“共享 > App”中撤销 HealthKit 授权。
  • 卸载 SwimTrace 会删除应用本身及其在你设备上保存的数据。
  • 你可以随时在 iOS 设置中关闭通知。

6. HealthKit usage restrictions

SwimTrace follows Apple's HealthKit requirements. Health data obtained through HealthKit is never used for advertising or marketing, is never sold, and is not used beyond providing health and fitness features inside the app.

6. HealthKit 使用限制
