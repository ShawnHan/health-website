HealthKit data used by Recova

Based on the current app implementation, Recova may read the following HealthKit data:

  • Heart rate
  • Resting heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV / SDNN)
  • Heartbeat series
  • Step count
  • Workouts
  • Active energy burned
  • VO2 Max
  • Body temperature
  • Body mass
  • Sleep analysis
  • Biological sex
  • Date of birth

How data is used

Recova uses available HealthKit records to display trends and generate training load, recovery, and readiness guidance inside the app.

The current version does not include third-party analytics, advertising, social login, or crash reporting SDKs. The project contains native StoreKit subscription support, and payment processing for App Store subscriptions is handled by Apple rather than directly by the developer.

If Recova changes how data is processed in a future version, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

数据如何使用

Recova 使用可用的 HealthKit 记录在应用内展示趋势，并生成运动负荷、恢复状态和训练准备度建议。

当前版本未接入第三方分析、广告、社交登录或崩溃统计 SDK。项目包含原生 StoreKit 订阅能力，通过 App Store 产生的订阅支付由 Apple 处理，开发者不会直接处理用户银行卡信息。

如果未来版本调整了数据处理方式，本隐私政策会同步更新。
