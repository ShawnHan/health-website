Core features

Core features

Three jobs, done well.

三件事，做到位。

Recova is not another step counter. It focuses on the three things athletes actually need to understand.

我们不做第十八个计步器。Recova 专注在训练者真正关心的三件事。

01

Load

Measure your training load

量化你的训练负荷

Using heart-rate reserve, duration, and a TRIMP model, each session becomes a comparable training-load score. Long-term trends stay legible at a glance.

基于心率储备、时长与 TRIMP 模型，将每一次运动折算为统一的训练负荷分数。长期趋势一目了然。

02

Recovery

Read recovery with context

读懂身体的修复状态

HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep stages are folded into a 0-100 recovery score, so you know before the next session whether the body is actually ready.

HRV、静息心率、睡眠分期融合成一个 0-100 的恢复分，让你在下一次训练前就知道身体是否准备好。

03

Guidance

Tell you the next move

给出下一步该做什么

Short term, it tells you whether today is for training, dialing back, or resting. Long term, it frames four-week blocks as Base, Build, Peak, and Recover.

短期告诉你今天练不练、练多重；长期按四周为周期建议 Base / Build / Peak / Recover。像有个教练在你手腕上。

Does Recova require a subscription?

Recova 需要付费吗？

The download is free. Core analysis can stay free, while deeper long-term trends and periodized guidance can gradually become subscription features.

下载免费。基础分析永久免费；更完整的长期趋势与周期化建议可以逐步作为订阅能力提供。

01

Does data get uploaded to your servers?

数据会上传到你们服务器吗？

The current product direction is centered on Apple Health permissions and on-device interpretation. The privacy page lists the HealthKit categories currently read and the present handling scope.

当前产品方向围绕 Apple Health 授权与本地信号解读展开。独立隐私页会列出当前会读取的 HealthKit 类别，以及现阶段的数据处理范围。

02

Can I use it without an Apple Watch?

没有 Apple Watch 可以用吗？

Not for now. Recova depends on continuous heart-rate, sleep, and workout signals from Apple Watch, and the phone alone cannot reach the same fidelity.

暂不支持。Recova 的核心依赖 Apple Watch 持续采集的心率、睡眠与运动数据，手机单机无法达到同等精度。

03

Which workout types are supported?

支持哪些运动类型？

Any workout already recorded through Apple Health can feed the load model, including running, cycling, swimming, strength training, hiking, yoga, and other mixed sessions.

凡是已经通过 Apple Health 记录下来的运动，都可以进入负荷判断，包括跑步、骑行、游泳、力量训练、登山、瑜伽以及更多混合型训练。
